Preparation Transfer

Before you begin, make sure you have:

  1. Have the California Certificate of Title on hand. If you do not have the title, complete an Application for Replacement or Transfer of Title (REG 227).
  2. Make sure the title has been signed by the buyer(s), seller(s), and lienholder (if applicable).
  3. Have the following:
    • Your driver’s license number
    • Vehicle license plate number
    • Vehicle identification number (VIN)
    • Legal owner (or lienholder) name and address
    • Vehicle make, model, and year
    • Purchase date and price
  4. Write the odometer mileage reading on the title (unless the vehicle is year model 2010 or older.
